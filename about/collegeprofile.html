College Vision

“To be a leading center of excellence in computer education, fostering a culture of innovation and research, producing technically competent and ethically responsible professionals who contribute to the advancement of the society and the nation.”


College Mission

  • “ To provide a world-class education that prepares students to be technically competent, innovative, and socially responsible professionals. ”
  • “ To foster a culture of excellence in teaching and research, foster industry -academia partnerships, and engage in community outreach activities that positively impact society. ”
  • “ To uphold high ethical standards and values in all aspects of the department’s activities, and to prepare students to be responsible and ethical professionals. ”
  • “ To cultivate a supportive and inclusive learning environment that promotes diversity, equity, and inclusion, and prepares students to work effectively in a global and multicultural society. ”
