Computer Engineering Department

From the Desk of HOD

Professor Sagar Rajebhosale is a member of the Syllabus Framing Committee for the Computer Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science branches at Savitribai Phule Pune University. With a strong focus on Information Security and Computer Engineering, he has contributed significantly to the field, having published 18 research papers. With a combined teaching experience of 12 years at esteemed institutions, he currently serves as the Head of the Computer Engineering Department.

Prof. Rajebhosale holds a bachelor’s and master’s degree with first-class honors from Savitribai Phule Pune University. Currently, he is pursuing his Ph.D. from the same institution. His areas of expertise and research interest encompass Information and Cyber Security, Blockchain Technology, and Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.
